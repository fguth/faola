Faola

Faola is a helpful tool for styling buttons in an easy and (really) fast way. You choose your button style and we give you the stylesheet+class (no images!) to apply all over your project. Yes, it's that simple.

Button style

You can choose Lightened of the four options of buttons. Each one have its own hover and active states style.

Border roundness

You can choose the roundness of your button from one of the options on the side. We do not allow custom values yet.

Base color

You can choose one of the colors or type in your own hex value. All the rest is dLightened by us. Feels like magic.

DLightened? Get your css file!

All you need to do now is to load the stylesheet on your project and apply the class "faola" wherever you want to see your button styled.
